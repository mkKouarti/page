Questions

What people ask before hiring me

What happens if I don't like the result?
You don't pay. You review the delivered work and if it doesn't serve you, you tell me. I issue no invoice, claim nothing, and you keep whatever already sits in your name.
How long does it take?
Between 1 and 7 days. A simple site lands in 1 or 2. A migration with mail and certificates runs to 5 or 7. The exact date goes in the scope document before I start.
How do I pay?
Outside this website. Bank transfer or Bizum once the work is delivered and you have approved it. This page takes no payments and stores no banking data.
Do you ask for a deposit?
No. I start with nothing up front. I carry the risk that you won't like it.
Is the website really mine?
Yes. The domain registers in your name, the code gets handed to you and the hosting sits in your account. If you want a different supplier tomorrow, you take everything without asking me.
What do you need from me to start?
The text you want to show, your logo if you have one, and access to the domain if you already bought it. If you have none of that, I write a first draft and you correct it.
Do you work on site?
I work remotely across Spain. In Madrid I can come in person when the job needs it, for example touching a server that isn't exposed to the internet.
I already have a website and I don't want to change it
Then migration or an audit is what you want. I can move your current site to a cheaper server or review where it sits exposed, without redoing the design.
Do you issue invoices?
Yes, with full tax details, and always after you approve the work.
What do you do with my data?
I keep your name and your contact so I can reply, nothing else. I sell no data, run no advertising and set no tracking. The privacy policy spells it out.
