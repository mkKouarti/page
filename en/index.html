Questions

What people ask before hiring us

What happens if I don't like the result?
You don't pay. You review it, you tell me it doesn't serve you and I close it there. I issue no invoice, claim nothing, and you keep whatever already sits in your name.
What if I need less than what is listed?
I take out what you won't use and the number drops. You get it in writing in the scope sheet, before I touch anything.
Do the prices include VAT?
Yes. The number you see on each service is the number you pay.
How long does it take?
One to seven days, and the clock starts the day I have all your material: text, logo and access. A simple site lands in two. A move with mail and certificates runs to five or seven. The exact date goes in writing before I start.
Can I ask for something that isn't on the list?
Yes. Tell me the task that eats your morning and I tell you whether it can be done, what it costs and when you get it.
I pay a monthly fee for a tool. Can you get rid of it?
Sometimes. I check whether an equivalent exists that you can host yourself. If it does, I set it up and charge what three months of that fee would cost. If it doesn't, I say so and charge nothing. I stay away from invoicing and anything with a legal obligation attached.
How do I pay?
Outside this website. Bank transfer or Bizum once the work sits in front of you and you have approved it. This page takes no payments and stores no banking data.
Do you ask for a deposit?
No. I start with nothing up front and carry the risk that you won't like it.
Is the website really mine?
Yes. The domain registers in your name, the code gets handed to you and the hosting sits in your account. Tomorrow you can take everything without asking me.
What do you need from me to start?
The text you want to show, your logo if you have one, and access to the domain if you already bought it. If you have none of that, I write a first draft and you correct it.
Where do you work?
Remotely, with clients in Spain and abroad. In Madrid I come in person when the job needs it, for example to touch a server that never reaches the internet.
I already have a website and I don't want to change it
Then look at the move or the review. I can shift what you have to a cheaper server, or tell you where it sits exposed, without touching the design.
Do you issue invoices?
Yes, with full tax details, always after you approve the work.
What do you do with my data?
I keep your name and your contact so I can reply, nothing else. I sell no data, run no advertising and set no tracking. The privacy policy spells it out.
